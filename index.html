The Mission

Transforming Students' Lives by the Gospel.

Kamp Love exists to reach college students with the message of the Gospel, providing an environment where they can grow spiritually, build meaningful relationships, and be equipped for lifelong discipleship.

Camping

In Camping, we find communion with God by adventuring out into His creation to seek refuge and retreat in Him.

Connection

In Connection, we look outside of ourselves to one another, bridging relationships that would have otherwise gone un-made.

Community

In Community, we give of ourselves to one another in deep and meaningful relationship, building on earth as it is in heaven as a family in Christ.

Christ

In Christ, we have everything we could ever need — the center, the reason, and the author of every Kamp.

The Origin

It started with a camping trip called Camp Lope.

Kamp Love started as a friend group camping trip at Grand Canyon University, fittingly titled “Camp Lope.” Out of one weekend in the pines grew a ministry that would travel to campuses across the country.

Jarod Lovekamp was a student at GCU who was instrumental in the growth and building of Camp Lope. On September 29, 2020, Jarod unexpectedly went to be home with the Lord. In his memory, Camp Lope became Kamp Love — a mission to change the world one Kamp at a time.

The Model

Fall trip. Spring retreat. Year-round leaders.

24 hour fall camping trip — Students disconnect from the noise and encounter Christ in His creation.

Full weekend spring retreat — A retreat that deepens community and sends students back to campus rooted in discipleship.

100% student lead — Student leaders are equipped on their campus all year to shepherd the next class of students into the Kamp Love community.
