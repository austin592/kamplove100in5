01 · Who We Are

A leader development organization.

We develop student leaders through six categories of wholistic discipleship: hard skills, soft skills, spiritual formation, content, opportunity, and coaching.

Students who go through our program leave campus equipped to lead in their churches, careers, and communities. We measure our work by what happens in their lives after the weekend ends.

That leadership is actualized through camping, connection, community, and Christ.

02 · Mission · Vision · Model

Three pillars, one direction.

Mission

What we do.

Transform students' lives by the gospel through camping, connection, community, and Christ.

Vision

Where we are going.

A world where students on every college campus are equipped in their gifts, making disciples who make disciples.

Model

How we do it.

24-hour fall camping trip. Full weekend spring retreat. 100% student-led on every campus.

03 · My Vision

What I believe God is calling us to build.

  1. Kamp Love at 100 universities in 5 countries, partnered with 100 local churches, by December 1, 2030.
  2. Develop 10,000 college student leaders through our 6-category discipleship framework.
  3. Create and deliver student leader development content, opportunity, and coaching that sets the standard for campus ministry.
  4. Own and operate retreat centers across the world, becoming the standard for excellence in camp and retreat ministry.
  5. Kamp Love Kids: curriculum development for kids camps, running Kamps for kids at and for churches.
  6. Kamp Love Summer Camp: for churches at summer camp locations and church campuses.
  7. Kamp Songs: equipping 1,000 young worship leaders to write and release original worship music for the Church.

04 · What We Have Done

Five years. Nineteen campuses. Eighteen thousand students.

A friend-group camping trip at Grand Canyon University grew into a national college camping ministry. We have reached 18,000 students and developed 5,550 student leaders across 19 active campuses.

09 · Departments

Seven departments. One body.

Storytelling

All digital communication: website, socials, brand book, and training of university leaders in media and art. The story told in many ways with excellent and innovative pieces of media, online and in person.

Finance & HR

Financial controller liaising between Steadfast (our financial services team) and HQ. HR and Legal Manager ensures excellent onboarding, off-boarding, legal compliance, and protection.

Development

All fundraising and partnership efforts. Director of Development plus a manager for each of the 5 categories: churches, orgs and businesses, parents, Kampers and alumni, and high net-worth individuals.

Integrity

Holds the organization accountable to do what we said we would do, when we said we would do it. Builds systems for, and tracks, productivity across the organization.

Leadership

Carries out the ministry and business leadership philosophy and DNA of the ministry. Builds the curriculum and training for student leader development across all 6 categories.

Kamps & Retreats

All university Kamp Loves and the management of state directors, regional directors, coaches, and student leader teams.

Kamp Songs

Writes, records, and releases original Kamp Love worship songs. Runs retreats to train, develop, and write with worship leads nationally. Carries the worship DNA of the ministry.

15 · Fundraising Strategy

Six categories. Five ways to participate. Five stages.

Six categories

Parents.

Churches.

Alumni.

Orgs & Businesses.

High Net-Worth Individuals.

Grants.

Five ways to participate

We invite people in through trackable participation. One star for each level; we aim for 5-star participants.

  1. Talk with us: email, phone, text, newsletter.
  2. Pray with us: prayer chain, prayer call.
  3. Serve with us: at a Kamp, at an event, with student leaders.
  4. Give to us: financially.
  5. Share us: referrals, posts, tell the story.

Five stages

  1. Identify: outbound communication through email, text, call.
  2. Qualify: by their level of participation (the 5-star system).
  3. Cultivate: ongoing communication and invitation via email, text, call, socials.
  4. Solicit: ask boldly and clearly for money and resources.
  5. Steward: innovative gratitude through gifts, trips, and thank-you's.
