01 · Who We Are

A leader development organization.

We are a leader development organization, with vision to develop the best student leaders in the world.
We develop world class leaders in 6 categories, our framework of wholistic discipleship: hard skills, soft skills, spiritual formation and content, opportunity and coaching.
We are on mission to see their leadership actualized in the transforming of lives by the gospel through camping, connection, community and Christ.
02 · Mission · Vision · Model

MISSION · VISION · MODEL

03 · My Vision

What I believe God is calling us to build.

  1. Kamp Love at 100 universities in 5 countries, partnered with 100 local churches, by December 1, 2030.
  2. Develop 10,000 of the 100,000 most influential college student leaders in the world.
  3. Create and utilize the best student leader development content, opportunity, and coaching in the world.
  4. Own and operate retreat centers across the world, becoming the standard for excellence in camp and retreat ministry through business and ministry excellence.
  5. Kamp Love Kids: curriculum development for kids camps, and running Kamps for kids at and for churches.
  6. Kamp Love Summer Camp: for churches at summer camp locations and church campuses.

04 · What We Have Done

Five years. Nineteen campuses. Eighteen thousand students.

From a friend-group camping trip at Grand Canyon University to a national college camping ministry, Kamp Love has reached 18,000 students and developed 5,550 student leaders across 19 active campuses. Our Southeast expansion proposal anchors a growing national footprint.

Five Ways To Participate

We invite people into the mission of Kamp Love through trackable participation. One star for each level of participation. We aim for 5-star participants.
  1. Talk with us: email, phone, text, newsletter.
  2. Pray with us: prayer chain, prayer call.
  3. Serve with us: at a Kamp, at an event, with student leaders.
  4. Give to us: financially.
  5. Share us: referrals, posts, tell the story.

Five Stages

  1. Identify: outbound communication through email, text, call.
  2. Qualify by their level of participation (the 5-star system).
  3. Cultivate: ongoing communication and invitation via email, text, call, socials.
  4. Solicit: asking boldly and clearly for money and resources.
  5. Steward: innovative and creative gratitude through gifts, trips, and thank-you's.
We have a world-class call center and support center of relationship managers who call partner participants monthly, pray with those God puts in our path, and continually make the invitation to others.
