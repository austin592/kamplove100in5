The Board · April 2026

The men and women who carry the mission with us.

Kamp Love is built by a body. The board provides governance, counsel, and covering — men and women of proven character whose lives have prepared them to steward this moment with us.

01 · Why The Board Matters

Counsel, covering, and accountability.

Kamp Love is a vehicle. The mission is the Lord's. The board exists so the founder, the staff, and the body of work are stewarded faithfully — spiritually, legally, financially, and strategically — through every season of growth.

Each member below brings a different gift to the table. Together they are the people God has built around this mission for such a time as this.

02 · The Board

Six leaders. One mission.

Kent Wisenor

Vice President of Sales · Bay Area, California

Kent leads enterprise sales for a cloud-native infrastructure company in the San Francisco Bay Area. With more than two decades selling, building, and coaching high-performing go-to-market teams in the technology industry, he brings a senior sales leader's instinct for clarity, accountability, and execution to Kamp Love. Kent and his family live in Danville, California.

Noah Wolfe

Sr. Director of Development, GCU · Phoenix, Arizona

Noah serves as Senior Director of Development at Grand Canyon University, where his career has been spent helping people invest in mission-driven Christian higher education. A GCU alumnus and former student body president, Noah moved from Discover team lead to senior development associate to Director of Alumni Relations, and today oversees fundraising for GCU's nine colleges and university-wide initiatives. He brings deep relational fluency, a fundraising operator's discipline, and a heart for college-aged disciples to the Kamp Love board.

Edy Sutherland

Disciple-Maker · Faith in the Outdoors

Edy is a disciple-maker who has built her ministry at the intersection of faith and the outdoors. Author of The WHEE Factor and a longtime voice on outdoor leadership and experiential discipleship, Edy uses wilderness adventure — the trail, the climb, the dive — as the classroom where students meet Christ. Her work mirrors the Kamp Love conviction that students' lives are transformed when they step off campus and into His creation. She brings a seasoned discipleship voice and a builder's heart for outdoor ministry to the board.

Skip Powell

Emergency Medicine Physician · Real Estate Entrepreneur

Skip is an emergency medicine physician and a real estate entrepreneur. He practices full-time in the ER and has built a parallel real estate investment portfolio that funds the next chapter of his life and giving. Earlier seasons of Skip's story included serving with Eagle Lake Camps, a Christian camping ministry; that camp DNA continues to shape how he reads the Kamp Love mission today. He brings clinical discipline, entrepreneurial range, and a long love for camp ministry to the board.

Tim Rockwell

Business Owner & Growth Consultant · Madison, Wisconsin

Tim is a business owner and growth consultant in Madison, Wisconsin. Across more than three decades in business he has founded, led, and advised companies of every size, and across the Madison business community he is regarded as one of its most consistent mentors and strategic minds. Tim brings deep operational wisdom, a strategist's eye for inflection points, and a long-view of leadership to Kamp Love.

Peter Swenson

Serial Entrepreneur · Founder & CEO of HIRE · Phoenix, Arizona

Peter is a serial entrepreneur and the Founder and CEO of HIRE, a Phoenix-based recruiting firm that builds scalable sales teams for door-to-door home service businesses. Before HIRE, Peter spent more than two decades selling, leading, and scaling field sales teams across the country, including as Co-Founder and CEO of Genesis Power Solutions and as Regional Manager and Divisional Vice President at NorthStar Home. He brings entrepreneurial instinct, recruiting and team-building expertise, and a builder's energy to the Kamp Love board.

03 · Board Structure

Four boards. One mission.

Kamp Love is governed by four complementary boards. Together they steward the legal, strategic, spiritual, and personal dimensions of the work.

Legal

Fiduciary and legal oversight: compliance, governance, protection.

Advisory

Strategic counsel from proven operators and ministry leaders.

Pastoral

Spiritual covering for staff, founder, and organizational health.

Founder's Advisors

Personal counsel to the founder on vision, family, and long-term stewardship.

The full four-board composition is still in formation. The leaders above represent the founding seats currently being seated across these boards.
