01 · What Kamp Songs Is

The worship DNA of the ministry.

Kamp Songs writes, records, and releases original Kamp Love worship songs. We run retreats to train, develop, and write with worship team leads from across the country. Then we record what the Lord gives us and send it out to serve churches and camps.

It is a discipleship pipeline for young worship leaders. Music is the byproduct of the formation work.

02 · The Rhythm

Write. Record. Release.

01

Write on retreat.

Songs are written during Kamp Songs retreats: co-written by worship leaders from Kamp Love campuses, guided by staff, tested live in the room before they are tracked anywhere.

02

Record live.

We record in the prayer chapel, live: one room, one take, one congregation. The sound carries the imprint of the moment it was made in, not the polish of a studio.

03

Release for the Church.

Songs go out on every major platform for free use by local churches, student ministries, and anyone building worship sets. The aim is usefulness, not streams.

03 · The Vision

One thousand young worship leaders.

1,000
Young worship leaders equipped to write and release original worship music for the Church. Formed on retreats, sent into their local contexts, carrying the Kamp Love worship DNA into the next generation.

That is the target. Live in the Prayer Chapel is the first long-form record. Everything that follows (retreats, tracks, relationships, stewardship) is in service of the one-thousand number.
